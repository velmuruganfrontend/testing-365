MyRx365 Workflow

Download the patients app from the AppStore

Register for an account using basic demographic info, along with your phone and email address

Lookup and establish care with your specific providers

Send requests for appointments to your provider through the app or via traditional means

Consent to a telemedicine visit and confirm your insurance status and demogprahics prior to the call.

Note: Any required payment will be collected by your provider's office

Upload any documents that you'd like to review with your doctor

Measure your vitals using FDA approved medical remote patient monitoring at your convenience or during a live video call

Answer video calls from your provider at the scheduled appointment window to discuss your issues

Receive prescriptions, lab/radiology orders, and specialist referrals through the app

Upon completion of the visit, review video of the entire encounter

Upon completion of the visit, review video of the entire encounter and print/attach the transcript to the patient's chart within your EHR of choice

Review the recorded encouter, and input CPT or ICD codes into conventional billing system to payors
