MyRx365 is an intuitive telehealth platform that allows physicians to connect with patients for routine appointments over secure video visit – and receive full reimbursement from most private payers. The telehealth platform allows doctors to offer remote patient monitoring, chronic care management, and behavioral health integration services easily. This is an ideal solution during pandemics such as COVID-19

With the MyRx365 platform, doctors can provide care to their patients remotely with the aid of peripheral remote patient monitoring devices that patients have pre-purchased. The doctor calls the patients at a scheduled time through the platform and the whole interaction gets recorded. If any prescriptions, lab, or radiology orders are required, they can also be placed.





Remote Patient Monitoring Devices

Patients can make a one-time purchase from a list of remote patient monitoring devices here – https://ihealthlabs.com

The TeleHealer platform currently supports the following types of remote patient monitoring devices – Blood Pressure, Pulse Oximeter, Weighing Scale, Thermometer, and Glucometers
